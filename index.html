Welcome

Thank You for taking your time to look at our website! Harlow Marina offers an idyllic place to stay and store your craft, long or short term. Walk around our grounds right before you have a bite to eat at our Clubhouse Cafe. Need more coal, fuel or even rope? Then why not visit our chandlery, the perfect stop for your floating, boating needs.

Facilities

Founded in 2022, our family run, modern site is located on the River Stort on a canal network that goes through London. The pefect spot to start your boating journey. Our 200 space, secure mooring site suitable for narrow and wide beam crafts up to 75ft has everything for you could possibly need.

  • Annual & visitor mooring
  • Pump out & elsan
  • Water fill up points
  • Chandlery
  • Calor gas
  • Coal, wood & kindling
  • Diesel
  • Clubhouse Cafe
